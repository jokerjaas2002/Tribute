Lionel Messi

The best Player of all Time

Here's a time line of LIONEL MESSI life:

  • 1987 - Born on June 24 in Rosario, Argentina.
  • 1995 - Joined the local club Grandoli at the age of 6.
  • 1997 - Moved to Newell's Old Boys, where he played until he was 13.
  • 2000 - Moved to Spain to join FC Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia.
  • 2004 - Made his first-team debut for FC Barcelona on October 16 against Espanyol.
  • 2005 - Scored his first goal for Barcelona on May 1 against Albacete.
  • 2009 - Won his first Ballon d'Or, becoming the youngest player to win the award at that time.
  • 2010 - Scored his first World Cup goal for Argentina against Serbia and Montenegro.
  • 2011 - Won the UEFA Champions League with Barcelona, scoring in the final against Manchester United.
  • 2012 - Set a new record for the most goals in a calendar year, scoring 91 goals.
  • 2014 - Led Argentina to the World Cup final, where they finished as runners-up to Germany.
  • 2015 - Won the UEFA Champions League and the FIFA Club World Cup with Barcelona.
  • 2019 - Won his sixth Ballon d'Or.
  • 2021 - Left FC Barcelona due to financial issues at the club and signed with Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) on August 10.
  • 2022 - Won the FIFA World Cup with Argentina, scoring two goals in the final against France.
  • 2023 - Joined Inter Miami CF in Major League Soccer (MLS) on July 15.
