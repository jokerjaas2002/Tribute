Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, agronomist and Nobel Peace Prize winner, known for his contributions to agriculture.

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who developed high-yielding, disease-resistant wheat varieties, which helped increase agricultural production and combat world hunger.
